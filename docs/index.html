The Internet

The internet is a vast place that much of society navigates on a daily basis. Honestly, what would we do without it? It's become such a staple in a human's life that many of us can say we can't live without it. From a large collection of memes that come and go, to the countless youtube videos of cats, we can say that the internet helps us in many ways.

Your Data

Despite the many advantages that the internet gives us, theres a very large con that is looming us everytime we open a new website. That is, the protection of our personal data and information is at risk whenever we are using the internet. It's important to understand these risks to know how to mitigate them.

What You Can Do

There are many ways you can go about protecting your identity online. Many strategies are discussed here. Please navigate the site to find out more about how to shield yourself from those pesky companies that want to know more than what is necessary or what you allow them to.
